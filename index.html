How does the ranked matchmaking matrix balance teams?

Our engine uses a calibrated algorithmic MMR balancing system. When a lobby forms, it analyzes the live Elo rating of all 10 players to distribute them mathematically. This prevents unbalances (like putting the 5 strongest players on the same side) and ensures perfectly mirrored, competitive, and fair matches.
