Frequently Answered Parameters

Why this price for the Pro License?

The €4.99 monthly subscription guarantees a premium, uninterrupted service. It covers a high-performance cloud hosting infrastructure with a 99.99% uptime framework, continuous stability & security updates to match Discord API evolutions, and permanent 24/7 technical staff support to assist your team at any time.

How does the ranked matchmaking matrix balance teams?

Unlike basic random bots, our engine uses a calibrated algorithmic MMR balancing system. When a lobby forms, it analyzes the live Elo rating of all 10 players to distribute them mathematically. This prevents unbalances (like putting the 5 strongest players on the same side) and ensures perfectly mirrored, competitive, and fair matches.

How fast is player voice migration handled?

It's instantaneous. As soon as teams form or a substitution executes, our pipeline batch-moves players simultaneously to avoid Discord API rate-limits.
